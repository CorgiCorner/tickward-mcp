# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or other protected status.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and considerate.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and learn from mistakes.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing another person's private information.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, trolling,
and deliberate disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate this policy
and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Report conduct
concerns privately through the repository's security reporting channel in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Maintainers will protect the reporter's privacy as
far as reasonably possible.

This code of conduct applies in project spaces and when representing the
project in public spaces.
